About

I graduated in Computer Science Engineering and obtained a Master's in Artificial Intelligence & Robotics from Sapienza University of Rome, where I also completed my PhD in 2026 within the AlcorLab group under the supervision of Prof. Paolo Russo — including a six-month research visit to the University of Sydney. I'm now continuing in the same group as a postdoctoral researcher.

My research focuses on deep learning, computer vision, and explainable AI, with applications spanning medical imaging, environmental forecasting, and visual analytics. Beyond the technical side, I'm interested in the philosophical questions that the field continues to raise.

Research

  • Medical AI
    Deep learning for cancer grading using Raman spectroscopy and wavelet transforms.
  • Environment
    Forecasting air quality and sea-surface temperature from open Copernicus data.
  • Explainable AI
    Using interpretability signals to guide knowledge distillation between models.
  • Visual Analytics
    Interactive environments for exploring high-dimensional financial data.
  • Cybersecurity
    Adversarial attacks and defences for LLMs in diagnostic settings; CAM manipulation for intellectual property protection of deep learning models.
